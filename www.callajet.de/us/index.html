Frequently asked questions

What does Empty leg flight mean?

According to experts, on average 30% of all charter flights and private jet flights are carried out without passengers, for example, when customers book a one-way flight on a private jet. So when a jet returns to its hub or flies to the next destination from the previous flight empty, it is called an Empty leg.
It gives companies a great opportunity to offer one-way flights at a discounted price whether it is a business trip or a private flight. Empty legs offer the luxury of hiring a private jet at a fraction of the original cost.

Why is it faster with a private jet?

If you are going on vacation with your family or you are having a private trip, you certainly don’t want to have a long wait at the security, check-in and baggage claim. With a private jet flight you can save your time and keep privacy skipping all the lines at the airport.
Chartering a private jet is an optimal solution for individual and group trips. By the way, you can take your dearest pet with you in the cabin and enjoy the flight together.
A private plane will skyrocket your business as it can take you and your business partners whenever and wherever you need with an access to smaller regional airports. The best private jets allow you and your team to relax or prepare for a meeting in the comfort of a luxury aircraft.

What does a private jet rental offer?

In short: plane rental means freedom, comfort, time saving, security and luxury. A private jet charter opens up new dimensions of travel: with a private jet charter you only need to arrive 15 minutes before take-off at a separate general aviation terminal without the buzz of the big airports. Once in the air your flight attendant prepares your favourite meals and drinks. Sit back and enjoy the flight with your family or team.

How much does it cost to charter a private jet?

The price depends on the distance traveled, the size of the plane, date & availability and the number of passengers. The private jet prices in our real time Jet Price calculator are estimated prices.
